About

My research focuses on light-assisted storage, WO₃/TiO₂/BDD heterojunctions, and in/operando characterization. I aim to intrinsically couple light harvesting with ion storage in a single electrode.

Projects

  • Controlled growth and application of nanocrystalline WO₃ — Synthesis and morphology control applied to NH₄⁺ storage (achieving 620 mWh·cm⁻², AFM under review).
  • Design and optimization of photo-assisted composite electrodes — Constructing WO₃/TiO₂ and WO₃/BDD heterojunctions, significantly improving energy density and cycling stability.
  • Flexible diamond electrode fabrication — Achieved uniform flexible diamond films, exploring applications in energy storage and sensors.
